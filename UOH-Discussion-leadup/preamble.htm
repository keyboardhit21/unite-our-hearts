Preamble    

Unite Our Heart   Discussion Group   

The purpose of the group is an expression of the overall purpose of UniteOurHeart.com, for responders to be partakers of the initial regathering efforts toward the Ten Tribes that characterize “the Work of Elijah” in the last days.

The emphasis in this regathering effort is on fostering the taking of the first steps of the return from the spiritual captivity of the Ten Tribes. To facilitate this return, UOH Discussion Group hopes to follow our father Yaakov’s injunction; “Gather yourselves together.” (Gen. 49:2). Though we may be scattered around the world at this time, we understand this directive as the order to build “communities” of those traveling in the same direction. 

Given our starting points where we woke up to who we are, we may be on diverse paths of return. However, we foster a convergence along the “straight paths of the Torah.” (Jer. 31:9)

To this end we foster honest discussion of spiritual issues that come up on this journey. Of utmost importance, we ask all to pray for Divine guidance for the resolution of those issues. The resolutions we may publish as part of OUH’s research project on answers to prayer.
