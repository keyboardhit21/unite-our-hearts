Instructions on How to Join a Discussion Group

Unite Our Heart used to have its own discussion group where readers could sign up themselves. Due to malevolent spam, the program could not be protected. Those wishing to be in a discussion group with fellow travelers on the homeward journey of the Returning Ten Tribes are asked to go to our sister site: http://www.shaar-israel.org.  Please check out the site, register for its notification list. Upon receiving a "Welcome Letter," please write a reply telling us why you wish to be in the discussion group. Then you will be placed into the discussion group.  Thank you for bearing with us in this reality of our times.
